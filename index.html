Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 30, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how this app ("the App") handles data when you use it. The App is designed as a decision-support tool, not a navigation system, and prioritizes local computation, transparency, and minimal data collection.

1. Overview

The App helps users evaluate tidal margin and related conditions in shallow or nearshore waters by combining publicly available environmental data with user-provided assumptions (such as vessel draft).

  • The App does not require user accounts or login.
  • Core calculations are performed locally on the device.
  • The App does not provide navigation, routing, or safety guarantees, and does not make decisions on behalf of the user.

2. Data You Provide

The App may process the following data only for on-device functionality:

  • Selected region or water area
  • Vessel draft or boat type assumptions
  • Time selections used for "what-if" analysis

This data is:

  • Used only to perform calculations and display results within the App
  • Stored locally on your device for convenience (e.g., returning user experience)
  • Not associated with your identity

3. Automatically Collected Data

3.1 Diagnostic and Error Information

To maintain stability and improve reliability, the App may collect limited diagnostic information when errors occur, such as:

  • Error messages or stack traces
  • App state at the time of failure
  • Information related to failed data requests (e.g., when external data sources are unavailable)

This information is collected only when necessary, and only for debugging and quality improvement purposes.

3.2 Device and Usage Information

The App itself does not intentionally collect personal information such as your name, email address, or precise location.

However, third-party analytics, crash reporting, or logging services (if used) may automatically collect certain technical information, which can include:

  • Device type and operating system version
  • App version
  • IP address or approximate network information
  • General usage or performance metrics

Such data collection is governed by the privacy policies of the respective third-party service providers.

4. Third-Party Services

The App relies on public data sources to function, including but not limited to:

  • NOAA Tides & Currents
  • NOAA Electronic Navigational Charts (ENC)
  • National Weather Service (NWS)

These data sources are used in read-only form and do not receive user-specific information from the App.

If third-party analytics or crash reporting services are used, they are integrated solely to:

  • Detect crashes or errors
  • Improve performance and reliability

The App does not sell user data or use it for advertising purposes.

5. Data Storage and Retention

  • User-selected settings and cached environmental data may be stored locally on your device to support offline use.
  • Diagnostic data is retained only as long as necessary to investigate and resolve technical issues.
  • The App does not maintain long-term user profiles or behavioral histories.

6. In-App Purchases and Subscriptions

The App may offer subscriptions or other in-app purchases in the future.

All payments are processed by Apple's App Store using your Apple ID. The App does not receive or store your payment information.

Subscription management and billing are handled entirely by Apple, subject to Apple's own privacy and payment policies.

7. Your Choices and Control

Because the App does not require accounts and minimizes data collection:

  • You may remove all locally stored data by deleting the App from your device.
  • Diagnostic data collection is limited to technical necessity and cannot be used to identify you personally.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. No personal information is knowingly collected from children.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated as the App evolves or as legal requirements change.

Any material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.

10. Disclaimer

The App provides decision-support information only based on user inputs and publicly available data. It does not replace professional judgment, local knowledge, or safe operating practices.

All decisions remain the responsibility of the user.

11. Contact

At this time, the App does not provide a direct contact or support channel. Future versions of the App may include a contact method, which will be reflected in an updated Privacy Policy.
