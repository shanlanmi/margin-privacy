## What this app does

Tide Clearance helps you estimate your water margin by comparing tide height with your draft.

It is designed as a reference tool for shallow water awareness.

---

## Disclaimer

This app is for informational purposes only.  
It does not replace official nautical charts, navigation systems, or professional judgment.

## Common Questions

### Does this app guarantee safe navigation?
No. It provides reference calculations only.

### Where does the data come from?
Publicly available tidal data sources.
